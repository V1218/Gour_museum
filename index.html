See The Glimpse

The Museum at Valley Campus, Sagar, is a captivating
destination that immerses visitors in the life and legacy
of Doctor Harisingh Gour. Open from Monday to Saturday,
10:00 AM to 6:00 PM, this enriching space showcases a
plethora of historical artifacts, fascinating exhibits,
and insightful details about the illustrious founder of
Dr. Harisingh Gour Vishwavidyalaya. Beyond its homage to
this visionary leader, the museum offers countless treasures
waiting to be discovered, making it a must-visit for history
enthusiasts and curious minds alike. Plan your visit and unravel
the stories that define this beautiful place.
