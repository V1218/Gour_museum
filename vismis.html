Vision & Mission

The vision of Gour Sangrahalaya

- Inspire curiosity and appreciation for history and culture.

- Preserve and showcase the legacy of Dr. Gour.

- Connect generations through a vibrant digital platform.

- Promote lifelong learning and cultural engagement.

The mission of Gour Sangrahalaya

- Highlight Dr. Gour's achievements, contributions, and artifacts.

- Offer immersive online experiences through storytelling and interactive exhibits.

- Provide educational resources to engage and inspire visitors.

- Preserve historical treasures for future generations.
